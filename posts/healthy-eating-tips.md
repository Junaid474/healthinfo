Eating healthy doesn't have to be complicated. Here are 10 simple tips to help you eat better.

1. **Eat more fruits and vegetables.** Aim for at least 5 servings a day.
2. **Choose whole grains.** Brown rice, quinoa, and whole wheat bread are better choices than refined grains.
3. **Limit added sugars.** Check food labels for hidden sugars.
4. **Drink plenty of water.** Stay hydrated throughout the day.
5. **Eat smaller portions.** Use smaller plates to help control portion sizes.
6. **Cook at home.** You have more control over ingredients when you cook your own meals.
7. **Don't skip breakfast.** A healthy breakfast kickstarts your metabolism.
8. **Snack smart.** Choose nuts, seeds, or fruit instead of chips or candy.
9. **Limit processed foods.** These are often high in salt, sugar, and unhealthy fats.
10. **Listen to your body.** Eat when you're hungry and stop when you're full.

## Conclusion

Small changes can lead to big results. Start incorporating these tips into your daily routine today!
